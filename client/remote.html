This uploads a video file into this project’s local videos/ folder. These videos become part of the normal shuffled TV playlist.

Branding & Lab Identity

FabLabTV uses the built-in branding/fablab.png logo unless your lab uploads its own logo. Uploaded logos are saved as branding/logo.png.

Upload lab logo

Transparent PNG works best. This replaces the custom TV logo without changing the default FabLabTV fallback.
